About our Web app

Our Web app is a simple way for users to determine what celestial weather events are happening globally at a date of the users choosing. On our homepage, the user can select today's date or any previous date to get a list of events for that selected date from NASA's own weather monitoring data. You can find out more about NASA's space weather models and their mission here!
